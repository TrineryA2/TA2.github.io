WHO I AM

I build web apps, prototype game concepts, and write fiction. I don't do "AI-generated" resumes. I do things that work, worlds that feel real, and code that's intentional.
I'm not a senior developer. I'm not pretending to be. I'm a beginner who actually builds things—bookshelves, games, libraries, stories, and design docs. That puts me ahead of most people stuck on tutorials.

WHAT I BUILD

▮ BOOKSHELF
WEB APP
An offline PWA library manager. Store books, track reading status, and view them on a 3D wooden shelf.
▮ PRIMARY
WEB APP
A color memory game. Memorize a color, recreate it using RGB sliders, and score based on accuracy.
▮ STUMBLE
WEB APP
A web randomizer that sends you to hidden, weird, and interesting corners of the internet.
▮ THE LOOP
GAME CONCEPT
A narrative-driven 3D platformer about breaking free from conformity and facing the consequences of freedom.
▮ CONCRETE SHIFT
GAME CONCEPT
A first-person rhythm survival game about a construction worker trapped in a concrete pipe filling with wet cement.
▮ ESCAPE THE SALE

▮ ANIMAL RIDER
